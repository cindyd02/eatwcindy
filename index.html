About Me

Welcome to eatwcindy! My name is Cindy Dong, a self-proclaimed foodie. I am a rising junior in college, studying computer science at the University of Virginia. Here, I combine my two of my many passions: coding + food! My love affair with food began at an early age, inspired by the vibrant flavors and cultural diversity of my upbringing. Growing up in China, I was never a picky eater. My 奶奶 always made the best meals. I didn't need alarm clocks to wake me up from my mid-afternoon slumber, I would simply be woken up by the delicious smell of my grandmother's cooking drifting through the air. With both of my parents working in the resturant as a kid, I have been accustomed to experimenting in the kitchen. From cooking up random recipes to seeking out hidden gems in local eateries, my taste buds are always on an adventure!
